Shopify Installation

Go to Online Store -> Themes. Look for the Upload Theme button. Use this to upload the provided zip file. Gallery-Shopify should show up under More Themes. Under the actions dropdown you can choose to Preview the theme. When you are happy with your customizations, you can then publish the theme to use it on your live store.
